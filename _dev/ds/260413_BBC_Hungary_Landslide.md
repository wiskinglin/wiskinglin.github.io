# Orbán era swept away by Péter Magyar's Hungary election landslide
- URL: https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cd9vg782kx7o
- Media: BBC
- Fetch Date: 2026-04-13

After 16 years at the helm, Viktor Orbán has been decisively defeated in Hungary’s general election. Péter Magyar, a former government insider turned opposition leader, has secured a landslide victory, promising a "new era" of transparency and European cooperation.

Magyar’s campaign focused on systemic corruption and the erosion of democratic norms under Orbán. The defeat is seen as a historic rebuke of Orbán’s "illiberal democracy" and a pivotal moment for the European Union.
