# US to blockade Iran's ports from Monday
- URL: https://www.bbc.com/news/live/cp9vm5ezxz4t
- Media: BBC
- Fetch Date: 2026-04-13

Washington has announced a maritime blockade of Iranian ports starting Monday, following the collapse of ceasefire negotiations. President Donald Trump stated he "doesn't care" if Tehran returns to the negotiating table, signaling a shift to a more aggressive stance.

Oil prices have surged past $100 per barrel in response to the news. The US military maintains it will not impede non-Iranian traffic in the Strait of Hormuz, but the potential for miscalculation remains high.
