# In Hungary, Orbán’s loss shows how populism can run out of road
- URL: https://edition.cnn.com/2026/04/12/europe/hungary-election-orban-loss-latam-intl
- Media: CNN
- Fetch Date: 2026-04-13

The recent electoral defeat of Viktor Orbán in Hungary marks a significant turning point in European politics. After over a decade in power, Orbán’s Fidesz party lost to a broad opposition coalition led by Péter Magyar.

Analysts suggest this loss demonstrates the limits of nationalist populism when faced with economic stagnation and corruption scandals. The result has sent shockwaves through the European Union, with many seeing it as a sign of a shift back towards liberal democracy in Central Europe.
