# Asha Bhosle: The sound of Bollywood dies aged 92
- URL: https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c6ppd0qdp1do
- Media: BBC
- Fetch Date: 2026-04-13

Asha Bhosle, the legendary playback singer who recorded over 12,000 songs for the Indian film industry, has passed away at 92. Her career, which began in the 1940s, spanned the golden age of Bollywood and beyond.

Bhosle was celebrated for her unique ability to bring life to diverse characters through her voice. Her death marks the end of an era in South Asian music, as fans across the globe mourn the loss of one of the world's most recorded artists.
