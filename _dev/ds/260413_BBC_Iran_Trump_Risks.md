# Trump's Strait of Hormuz blockade threat raises risks
- URL: https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c8dl5mly2rzo
- Media: BBC
- Fetch Date: 2026-04-13

President Trump’s threat to blockade the Strait of Hormuz has introduced a high-stakes gamble into the ongoing US-Iran conflict. Military analysts suggest that while the US has the naval superiority to enforce such a blockade, Iran’s asymmetric capabilities—including mines and missile batteries—pose a significant threat to global shipping.

The blockade is intended to cripple Iran’s economy, but critics argue it may instead drive Iran towards more radical actions and drag the US into a protracted regional war with no clear exit strategy.
