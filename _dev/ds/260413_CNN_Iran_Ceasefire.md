# Live updates: Iran war; Trump says US-Iran ceasefire ‘holding well,’ US will blockade Strait of Hormuz
- URL: https://edition.cnn.com/2026/04/12/world/live-news/iran-us-war-talks-trump
- Media: CNN
- Fetch Date: 2026-04-13

President Donald Trump on Sunday declared that the ceasefire between the United States and Iran is "holding well," but emphasized that the US will maintain a blockade on the Strait of Hormuz to "ensure freedom of navigation" and "apply maximum pressure" until a comprehensive nuclear deal is reached.

The announcement came after a tense weekend of diplomatic talks in Zurich. Experts warn that the blockade could further escalate tensions in the region, affecting global oil prices.
