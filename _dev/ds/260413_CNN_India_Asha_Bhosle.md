# Indian singer Asha Bhosle dies at 92, ending ‘extraordinary’ journey
- URL: https://edition.cnn.com/2026/04/12/asia/asha-bhosle-death-intl
- Media: CNN
- Fetch Date: 2026-04-13

The world of Indian music is in mourning today following the death of legendary playback singer Asha Bhosle at the age of 92. Known for her incredible range and versatility, Bhosle recorded thousands of songs over a career spanning seven decades.

From Bollywood classics to pop songs, her voice defined generations of Indian cinema. Tributes are pouring in from around the globe, celebrating her as one of the most influential cultural icons of modern India.
