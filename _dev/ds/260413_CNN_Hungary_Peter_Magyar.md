# Who is Péter Magyar, the candidate who defeated veteran Hungarian Prime Minister Orbán?
- URL: https://edition.cnn.com/2026/04/12/europe/hungary-peter-magyar-profile-latam-intl
- Media: CNN
- Fetch Date: 2026-04-13

Péter Magyar, once an insider within Viktor Orbán’s political circle, has emerged as the unlikely hero for Hungary’s opposition. A former diplomat and lawyer, Magyar broke ranks with Fidesz early last year, exposing internal scandals and galvanizing the public.

His charismatic leadership and focus on transparency and rule of law resonated with voters tired of Orbán’s long reign. This profile explores his background, his motivations for turning against his former mentor, and what his victory means for the future of Hungary.
