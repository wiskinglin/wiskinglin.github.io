# What happened when a tiny propellor plane met a US aircraft carrier in a warzone
- URL: https://edition.cnn.com/2026/04/12/middleeast/us-iran-war-propellor-plane-intl-hnk-ml-dst
- Media: CNN
- Fetch Date: 2026-04-13

In a bizarre and potentially dangerous incident in the Gulf of Oman, a small civilian propeller plane made an emergency landing near a US carrier strike group. The plane, flying a routine route, suffered engine failure and navigated close to the USS Gerald R. Ford.

Navy officials reported that the plane was intercepted by fighter jets but allowed to ditch safely in the sea, where the crew was rescued by US sailors. The incident highlights the high tension and complex maritime environment in the region during the ongoing US-Iran standoff.
