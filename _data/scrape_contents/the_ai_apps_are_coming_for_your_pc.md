# The AI Apps are Coming for Your PC: OpenAI Codex and Gemini for Mac

This isn't a future prediction—it's happening now. The latest Verge "Installer" highlights how AI tools are moving beyond the browser to take over your PC's hardware and rewrite your daily workflows.

## AI is No Longer Just a Browser Tab
If you're still switching between AI chats in a browser, you're falling behind. The new trend is "System-Native AI" that lives in your OS and can even control your computer for you.

## Top AI Tool Highlights
• **OpenAI Codex** → OpenAI’s latest "all-in-one AI superapp." It’s more than a chatbox; it includes a browser, new coding tools, and a powerful setting that allows Codex to "directly use your computer for you."
• **Gemini for Mac** → Google’s official move to bring Gemini to the Mac system level. Despite some shortcut conflicts, it’s currently the fastest way to access Gemini, Google Drive, and Photos directly from your desktop.
